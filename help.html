Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Account & Security

How do I create an account?

Account creation is typically handled through our main platform. If you're accessing the wallet, you should already have an account. For new registrations, please visit our primary website or contact support.

How can I change my password?

You can change your password by clicking on your user profile icon in the top right corner of the wallet, then selecting "Change Password". A password reset link will be sent to your registered email address.

What should I do if I suspect unauthorized activity on my account?

If you suspect any unauthorized activity, please change your password immediately and create a support ticket with our team.

Deposits & Withdrawals

How do I deposit USDT?

Click the "Deposit" button on the main wallet page. This will take you to the deposit page where you'll find instructions and your unique deposit address for USDT (TRC20).

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawal processing times generally range from 30 minutes to 24 hours. While most withdrawals are completed within approximately one hour, the exact duration can vary based on the chosen method (IMPS/NEFT or USDT-TRC20) and prevailing network conditions or banking system latencies.

Are there any withdrawal fees?

Withdrawal fees may apply and can vary based on the method and network conditions. Any applicable fees will be displayed before you confirm your withdrawal.

What if I withdraw to the wrong address/account?

It is crucial to double-check all withdrawal details (wallet addresses, bank account numbers, IFSC codes) before confirming any transaction.

Due to the nature of cryptocurrency transactions and banking processes:

  • Cryptocurrency (USDT-TRC20): Transactions on the blockchain are generally irreversible. If you send USDT to an incorrect TRC20 address, it is highly unlikely that the funds can be recovered unless you know the owner of that address and they are willing to return the funds. GainX does not have control over external wallet addresses.
  • Bank Transfers (IMPS/NEFT): Once a bank transfer is requested, the details are submitted to our automated payment portal. This portal collaborates with our partnered financial institutions to process the transfer, aiming to ensure users receive funds that are clear and not subject to any complaints or liens. If incorrect bank account details are provided, the transaction might fail. In such cases, funds may eventually be returned to your GainX wallet, though this process can take several days or even weeks and is subject to standard banking procedures. However, if the incorrect details match an existing, valid bank account, the funds might be credited to that unintended account. Please note that once a withdrawal request has been processed through our company portals, initiating a chargeback or reversal is not possible from our end. Recovery in such scenarios is exceptionally challenging and not guaranteed.

User Responsibility: You are solely responsible for ensuring the accuracy of the withdrawal information you provide. GainX cannot be held liable for losses incurred due to withdrawals made to incorrect or unintended addresses/accounts provided by you.

If you realize you have made an error, please create a support ticket immediately with all relevant transaction details. While we cannot guarantee recovery, we will provide any guidance possible based on the situation.

Swapping Tokens

How do I swap tokens (e.g., USDT to ZSG)?

Click the "Swap Tokens" button. In the swap modal, select the token you want to swap from and the token you want to receive. Enter the amount, review the rate and approximate amount you'll receive, then confirm the swap.
